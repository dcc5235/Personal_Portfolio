Design Concept

When I thought about creating my portfolio site, I knew I wanted to encapsulate my personality within the design. I also wanted my portfolio to give off the energy of a comfortable, safe space where users can explore parts of who I am as a developer.

Colors: I chose more neutral, earthy tones to represent myself as a down to earth person. Although I like to plan my work 3 weeks to 3 months ahead of schedule, I like to leave room for last minute changes, self-reflection, and time with those I love.

Theme: I decided to utilize minimalist design concepts for two reasons. Firstly, less is more. Secondly, I wanted users to know that what they see before them is what's on the table. Transparency is important to me.

Images: I selected photos which showcased a home-environment. It was not only purposefully done to create a comfortable vibe, it also represents me — a caffeine fiend & a do-er, working from home.
