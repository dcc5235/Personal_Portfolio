About me

My name is Dany Chheang (she/her). I am a Front-End Developer with experience working in a fast-paced environment and with cross-functional teams. My top talent themes include Restorative (problem solve & implement solutions), Input (collect & archive), Developer (cultivate potential in others), Connectedness (see links between events), and Relator (form close relationships).

I am driven to find solutions that help people, which is why I am passionate about creating user-friendly interface that is inclusive, easily-accessibile, and impactful. In my spare time, I love to bike, travel, and binge-watch Seinfeld.
